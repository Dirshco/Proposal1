Join our innovative training initiative for IMGs in the US healthcare system.

Program Overview

The Clinical Research Fellowship (CRF) Pilot Program is a remote, part-time research and training initiative designed for International Medical Graduates (IMGs) with an interest in geriatrics and long-term care.

  • Remote and part-time commitment
  • Focus on long-term care, telemedicine, and geriatrics
  • Training in research, quality improvement, and US healthcare systems

Who Should Apply?

We welcome applications from:

  • International medical graduates (IMGs)
  • Recent residency applicants and unmatched graduates
  • IMGs with a strong interest in geriatrics and telehealth
